FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Pakistan Kidney Institute is a sister organization of PKLI, which was developed in year 2004 in the premises of Shifa International Islamabad. Apart from taking part in many relief and awareness programs, the institute has developed the best residency program in the country and has treated more than 100,000 patients. However, due to the increasing burden of kidney and liver diseases in the country, the institute is now being expanded to Pakistan Kidney and Liver Institute & Research Center.

Approximately 40% of all the diseases in Pakistan have kidney, bladder, and liver related origin. According to WHO, diseases related to the liver is the 11th leading cause of deaths in Pakistan with kidney diseases on number 12. The menace of Hepatitis B and C has turned into an epidemic in Pakistan. The country has the highest prevalence of Hepatitis C in the world after Egypt. The treatment of such diseases is either too expensive in the country or at times not available resulting in loss of innumerable precious lives. PKLI aims to put an end to this misery by providing state of the art, free of cost ingenious treatment, especially to the poor of the country.

Disease prevention and awareness programs are one of the main objectives of PKLI. The institute aims to curtail the existing burden of diseases through treatment, relief programs, awareness programs and preventive programs by dedicating required amount of funds to these heads.

Apart from treatment and prevention, the hospital aims to impart world class education and training to the future doctors in the country. The hospital construction and operations will be a source of employment for some 5000 labor and their families. As the project is being built on JCI standards, it will be a benchmark in the health care sector of Pakistan for others to follow.

The Hospital Complex is being built on a 50 acre land and will consist of hospital buildings (patient towers, emergency theaters, operation theaters, etc.), University, Mosque, Energy Center, Nursery, Male and Female Dormitories, Residential Area, PKLI Bazaar and Industrial Unit.
